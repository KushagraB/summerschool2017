Registration

Steps to Register(Please complete all the steps).

  1. Please read the Fee Details table (Table 1) below and calculate the payment you need to make.
  2. For instance if you are a female and want to live for two days in a single occupancy room your charge would be Rs 2300 + Rs 400 * 2 = Rs 3100.
  3. Transfer the money by using the details in Bank Details Table (Table 2).
  4. Fill the form(Form 1) once the payment is done.

Table 1. Fee Details

    Type Details Fee
    Registration Same for both Industry/Academic Participants Rs. 2000 + 15% service tax (2300)
    Hostel Single occupancy room with attached bath for Gents (Kadamba Nivas) Rs. 500/- per day
    Hostel Single occupancy room with attached bath for Ladies (Parijat Nivas) Rs. 400/- per day
    Hostel Shared room for Boys (Bakul Nivas) Rs. 300/- per day
    Hostel Shared room for Girls (Parul Nivas) Rs. 300/- per day

    *Choice of room picked cannot be guaranteed -- will be subject to availability.
